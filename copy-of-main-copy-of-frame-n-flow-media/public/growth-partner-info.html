Frame n Flow Media - Growth Partner Program

Program Type: Performance-Based Partnership
Commission: 20-30% of total deal value
Location: Fully Remote
Experience Required: None (Training Provided)

What is the Growth Partner Program?

The Growth Partner Program is a commission-based partnership opportunity where individuals connect qualified business leads with Frame n Flow Media and earn a percentage of the deal value when those leads convert into paying clients.

How It Works

  1. Outreach: Partners conduct cold outreach to potential clients via Instagram, LinkedIn, email, or phone
  2. Qualification: Identify businesses that need marketing services (product-based, service-based, startups, realtors)
  3. Appointment Booking: Schedule discovery calls between qualified prospects and Frame n Flow Media
  4. Sales Participation: Join the sales call to support the presentation
  5. Earn Commission: Receive 20-30% of the total contract value when the client signs

Commission Structure

  • Rate: 20-30% of total deal value
  • Variation: Commission percentage varies based on deal size and service complexity
  • No Cap: Unlimited earning potential - the more qualified leads you bring, the more you earn
  • Tracking: Transparent commission tracking via Partner Portal dashboard
  • Payment: Commissions paid after client payment is received

What You'll Do

  • Conduct cold outreach on social media platforms (Instagram, LinkedIn)
  • Send personalized messages to potential clients
  • Qualify leads based on budget, need, and decision-making authority
  • Schedule and confirm discovery call appointments
  • Participate in sales presentations
  • Log daily activity in the Partner Portal
  • Build relationships with prospects

What Frame n Flow Media Handles

  • All service delivery (marketing campaigns, AI visuals, automation)
  • Client onboarding and project management
  • Invoicing and payment collection
  • Customer support and account management
  • Contract negotiations and legal agreements

Requirements

  • Strong written and verbal communication skills
  • Ability to identify and qualify business leads
  • Professional presentation skills for client calls
  • Self-motivated and results-driven mindset
  • Consistent daily outreach activity
  • No prior marketing experience required - full training provided

What We Provide

  • Training: Complete sales training, outreach scripts, and qualification frameworks
  • Partner Portal: Dashboard for tracking leads, earnings, and performance
  • Materials: Marketing collateral, presentation decks, and case studies
  • Support: Direct access to founder Bhanu Deep for guidance
  • Tools: Lead management system, CRM integration, analytics
  • Resources: Library of scripts, templates, and best practices

Target Clients

Growth Partners focus on connecting with:

  • Product-Based Businesses: E-commerce, retail, consumer goods
  • Service-Based Businesses: Consultants, agencies, professional services
  • Startups: Early-stage companies needing growth systems
  • Realtors: Real estate agents and brokerages

Application Process

  1. Submit application via the Mission Brief form at framenflowmedia.in/growth-partner/apply
  2. Provide details about your background, outreach experience, and motivation
  3. Applications reviewed within 48 hours
  4. Approved partners receive access credentials to the Partner Portal
  5. Complete onboarding training and begin outreach

Partner Portal Features

  • Lead Tracking: Log and manage all prospects in one place
  • Commission Calculator: Real-time earnings estimator
  • Daily Logging: Track outreach activity and maintain streaks
  • Analytics: Performance metrics, conversion rates, and trends
  • Earnings History: View pending, approved, and paid commissions
  • Resource Library: Access scripts, guides, and training materials

⚠️ Important Clarification

This is NOT a "Get Qualified Leads or 100% Refund" offer.

That guarantee is part of Frame n Flow Media's client service offering - where we promise to deliver qualified leads to businesses who hire us for marketing services, or they get their money back.

The Growth Partner Program is a separate opportunity for individuals who want to earn commission by connecting qualified business leads with Frame n Flow Media.
