# Frame n Flow Media - Growth Partner Program

## Overview
Frame n Flow Media's Growth Partner Program is a performance-based partnership where individuals connect qualified business leads with our agency and earn commission on successful conversions.

## How It Works
Growth Partners:
1. Conduct cold outreach to potential clients (product-based businesses, service-based businesses, startups, and realtors)
2. Schedule discovery calls with qualified prospects
3. Participate in sales presentations alongside Frame n Flow Media team
4. Earn commission when clients sign contracts

Frame n Flow Media handles:
- All service delivery
- Client onboarding
- Project execution
- Payment processing

## Commission Structure
- Partners earn 20-30% commission on total deal value
- Commission rates vary based on deal size and complexity
- No cap on earnings - unlimited earning potential
- Transparent tracking through Partner Portal

## Requirements
- Strong communication and outreach skills
- Ability to identify and qualify business leads
- Professional presentation skills for client calls
- Self-motivated and results-driven mindset
- No prior marketing experience required (full training provided)

## What We Provide
- Complete sales training and outreach scripts
- Access to Partner Portal dashboard
- Marketing materials and presentation decks
- Direct support from founder Bhanu Deep
- Transparent commission tracking system
- Lead management tools
- Performance analytics

## Application Process
1. Submit application through Mission Brief form
2. Provide background, outreach experience, and motivation
3. Applications reviewed within 48 hours
4. Approved partners receive Portal access credentials
5. Begin onboarding and training

## Partner Portal Features
- Lead tracking and management system
- Real-time commission calculator
- Daily activity logging
- Performance analytics and streak tracking
- Resource library with scripts and guides
- Earnings history and payout tracking
